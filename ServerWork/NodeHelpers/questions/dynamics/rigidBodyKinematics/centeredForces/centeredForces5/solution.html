The first step is to draw a free-body diagram. Don't forget that there are 4 tires in total.

Summing forces to solve for unknowns:

  • $\Sigma{F}_x = ma ~\implies~ ma = 2F_T$
  • $\Sigma{F}_y = 0 ~\implies~ 0 = 2N_r+2N_f - mg$
  • $\Sigma{M}_G = 0 ~\implies~ 0 = 2N_r(L_r) - 2N_f(L_f) -2F_t(h) = 0$

    • Since we are slipping $2F_T = 2\mu_k N_r$

    Here we have 3 equations and three unknowns: $N_r,~N_f,$ and $a$

      Solving for acceleration we get: $a = \frac{2~\mu_k~ g ~L_f}{(L_r+L_f) - \mu_k h} = {{correct_answers.a}}~m/s^2$
