To determine the extension of the wire, we need to use the relationship between strain and extension. Strain is the ratio of extension to the original length of the wire.

Level 1

The formula for strain is given by:

Level 2

\( \) \text{Strain} = \frac{\Delta L}{L} \( \)

Level 3

Since the percentage strain is given, we can express the strain as a decimal by dividing by 100:

Level 4

\( \) \text{Strain} = \frac{ 4 }{100} \( \)

Level 5

Substituting the strain expression into the strain formula, we can solve for the extension \( \Delta L \):

Level 6

\( \) \Delta L = \text{Strain} \times L \( \)
