To calculate the monthly loan payment, we will use the formula for the monthly payment of an amortizing loan:

Level 1

The formula is given by: \[ A = P \frac{i(1 + i)^n} {(1 + i)^n - 1} \] where:

  • A is the total monthly payment
  • P is the loan principal (the amount borrowed)
  • i is the monthly interest rate (annual interest rate divided by 12)
  • n is the number of payments (loan term in months)

    • Level 2

    The loan principal can be calculated as: \[ P = \text{ (purchasePrice) } - \text{ (downPayment) } = 30017~ USD - 18175~ USD \]

    Level 3

    Level 4

    Level 5
