Level 0

To calculate the taxable income for ACME Inc., we start with the Before-Tax-and-Loan Cash Flow and deduct the payments made on loan principal, loan interest, and the MACRS depreciation deduction.

Level 1

The formula for taxable income can be expressed as: \[ \text{Taxable Income} = \text{Before-Tax-and-Loan Cash Flow} - \text{Loan Interest Payment} - \text{MACRS Depreciation Deduction} \]

Level 2

Substituting the known values: \[ \text{Taxable Income} = 9681 - 272.1 - 4298 \]

Level 3

The final taxable income for ACME Inc. is thus calculated as follows: \[ \text{Taxable Income} = 9681 - 272.1 - 4298 = 5110.9 \]

Level 4

The tax payed is the taxable income multiplied by the tax rate.

Tax Payed = 5110.9 \( \times \) 17%= 868.85

Level 5

The after tax cash flow (ATCF) can be found as: ATCF = BTLCF - Tax Payed - Expenses

In our case the expenses is just the principle payed.

AFTC = 9681 - 868.85 - 1814 = 6998.15
