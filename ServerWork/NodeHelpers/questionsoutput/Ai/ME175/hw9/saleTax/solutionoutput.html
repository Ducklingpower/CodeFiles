Level 1

The MACRS depreciation method allows for accelerated depreciation, which means that more depreciation expense is recognized in the early years of the asset's life. The property class determines the applicable depreciation rates.

Level 2

For a 7-year property, the MACRS rates can be found in the IRS guidelines. Typically, the rates for a 7-year property are approximately: 14.29%, 24.49%, 17.49%, 12.49%, 8.93%, 8.92%, 8.93%, and 4.46%.
