To determine the month in which the interest portion of the mortgage payment is less than one third of the total monthly payment, we need to first calculate the monthly payment using the mortgage formula.

Level 1

The formula for the monthly mortgage payment is given by: \[ A = P \frac{i(1 + i)^n}{(1 + i)^n - 1} \] where:

  • \( A \) is the total monthly payment
  • \( P \) is the principal (loan amount)
  • \( i \) is the monthly interest rate
  • \( n \) is the number of payments (loan term in months)
