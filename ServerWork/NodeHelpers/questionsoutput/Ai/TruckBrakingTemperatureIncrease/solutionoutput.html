Level 0

Problem Statement: The objective is to calculate the temperature increase in each brake drum of a truck when it comes to a stop. The truck weighs 2689 lb, is traveling at 62 mph, and must stop within 300 feet. The truck has 34 tires, each with a brake drum of 16.5 inches in diameter and weighing 131 lb. The specific heat of the brake material is 0.12 BTU/(lb*°F), and it is assumed that there is no heat transfer from the brakes to the outside during braking.

Level 1

Known Variables Description:

  • Weight of the truck (W): 2689 lb
  • Initial velocity (v_i): 62 mph (converted to feet per second for calculations)
  • Number of tires (n_tires): 34
  • Weight of each brake drum (m): 131 lb
  • Specific heat of brake material (c): 0.12 BTU/(lb*°F)

Level 2

Equation Setup:

  1. Kinetic energy (KE) of the truck is converted to thermal energy (Q) in the brakes: \(KE = \frac{1}{2} m v^2\) \(Q = KE\) (since energy is conserved and all kinetic energy is assumed to be converted to heat)
  2. The thermal energy absorbed by each brake drum is: \(Q_{\text{per brake} } = \frac{Q}{n_{\text{tires} } }\)
  3. The temperature change (\(\Delta T\)) in each brake drum is given by: \(Q = m c \Delta T\)

Level 3

Solution Process:

  1. Convert the truck's initial velocity from mph to ft/s: \(v_i = 62 \, \text{mph} \times \frac{5280 \, \text{ft} }{1 \, \text{mile} } \times \frac{1 \, \text{hr} }{3600 \, \text{} }\)
  2. Calculate the kinetic energy (KE) of the truck: \(KE = \frac{1}{2} m v_i^2\) where m is the weight of the truck in lb.
  3. Calculate the thermal energy (Q) absorbed by all brakes: \(Q = KE\)
  4. Calculate the thermal energy per brake drum (\(Q_{\text{per brake} }\)): \(Q_{\text{per brake} } = \frac{Q}{n_{\text{tires} } }\)
  5. Calculate the temperature increase (\(\Delta T\)) for each brake drum: \(\Delta T = \frac{Q_{\text{per brake} } } {m c}\)
  6. Add the temperature increase to the initial temperature (assumed to be the ambient temperature) to find the final temperature (\(T_f\)): \(T_f = T_i + \Delta T\)
