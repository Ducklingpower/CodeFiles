Level 0

Convert Celsius to Kelvin using the conversion formula.

Level 1

The conversion from Celsius to Kelvin is given by the formula: \( T_{K} = T_{°C} + 273.15 \).

Level 2

For part (a), use \( T_{°C} = 23°C \).

Level 3

Calculate: \( T_{K, a} = 23 + 273.15 = 296.15 \text{ K} \).

Level 4

For part (b), use \( T_{°C} = 32 °C \).

Level 5

Calculate: \( T_{K, b} = 32 + 273.15 = 305.15 \text{ K} \).
