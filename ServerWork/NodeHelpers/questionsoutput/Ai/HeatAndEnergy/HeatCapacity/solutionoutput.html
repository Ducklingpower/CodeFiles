Level 0

Write the definition for specific heat capacity.

Level 1

The specific heat capacity \( c_p \) is defined as the amount of heat \( Q \) required to change the temperature of a mass \( m \) by a temperature difference \( \Delta T \). It can be expressed as: \[ c_p = \frac{Q}{m \times \Delta T} \]
