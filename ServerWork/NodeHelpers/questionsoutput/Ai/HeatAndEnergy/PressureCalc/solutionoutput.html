Level 0

To find the pressure at volume \( V_2 \), we can use Boyle's Law, which states that for a given mass of gas at constant temperature, the product of pressure and volume is constant: \( P_1 V_1 = P_2 V_2 \).

Level 1

Rearranging the equation gives us: \( P_2 = \frac{P_1 V_1}{V_2} \).

Level 2

Thus, substituting the known values: \( P_2 = \frac{ 148 \cdot 7 }{ 9.42 } \)
