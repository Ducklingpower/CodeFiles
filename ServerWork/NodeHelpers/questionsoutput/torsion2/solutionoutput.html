Level 0

compute the torque acting in each section of the beam.

Level 1

In section AB torque = \( \tau_A \) , section BC torque = \( \tau_A-\tau_B \), and section CD torque = \( \tau_A-\tau_B + \tau_C \)

now use these torques to calcuate the twist in each section.

The equation of twist = \( \frac{\tau ~ L}{J G} \)

G = \( 75*10^9 \)

J = \( \frac{\pi}{2}(R^4) \)

Level 2

    Solving twist:
  • Twist in sectuion AB = \( \frac{\tau_A ~ L_1}{G ~ J} \)
  • Twist in sectuion BC = \( \frac{(\tau_A - \tau_B) ~ L_2}{G ~ J} \)
  • Twist in sectuion CD = \( \frac{(\tau_A - \tau_B + \tau_C) ~ L_3}{G ~ J} \)
    • Now you easily solve both of the questions by adding up the twist in each section.
