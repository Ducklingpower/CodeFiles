\( 0 = \dot{Q} - \dot{W} + \dot{m} \left( h_1 + \frac{V_1^2}{2} \right) - \dot{m} \left( h_2 + \frac{V_2^2}{2} \right) \)

Rearrange to solve for the heat transfer rate \( \dot{Q} \):

\( \dot{Q} = \dot{W} - \dot{m} \left[ h_2 - h_1 + \frac{1}{2} \left( V_2^2 - V_1^2 \right) \right] \)
