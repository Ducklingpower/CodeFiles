(a) To determine the ratio of mass flow rates of cooling water to steam (\( \dot{m}_3/\dot{m}_1 \)):

  • Apply the energy balance for the condenser: \( 0 = \dot{Q} - \dot{m}_1(h_1 - h_2) \)
  • From steam tables, find \( h_1 \) using \( P_1 \) and \( x_1 \): \( h_1 = 2584.7x_1 + 191.83(1 - x_1) \).
  • Find \( h_2 \) using \( P_2 \) and \( T_2 \): \( h_2 = h_f(T_2) \).
  • For cooling water, \( q_{cooling} = \dot{m}_3(h_4 - h_3) \).
  • Using \( h_3 = c_p T_3 \) and \( h_4 = c_p T_4 \), solve for \( \dot{m}_3/\dot{m}_1 \):
  • \( \frac{\dot{m}_3}{\dot{m}_1} = \frac{h_1 - h_2}{h_4 - h_3} \)
