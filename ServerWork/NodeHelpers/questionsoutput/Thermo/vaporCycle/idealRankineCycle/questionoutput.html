The net power output of the cycle is \( \mathbf{W_{net} } = 91~MW \).

  • (a) The thermal efficiency, \( \eta \).

  • (e) The rate of heat transfer, \( \dot{\mathbf{Q_{out} } } \), from the condensing steam as it passes through the condenser (in \( \mathbf{MW} \)).
  • (f) The mass flow rate of the condenser cooling water, \( \dot{\mathbf{m_{cw} } } \) (in \( \mathbf{kg/h} \)), if cooling water enters the condenser at \( \mathbf{T_{cw,in} } = 8~^{\circ}C \) and exits at \( \mathbf{T_{cw,out} } = 8~^{\circ}C \).
