Level 0

Start by applying the First Law of Thermodynamics for the open system.

Level 1

  • Apply the steady-state energy equation to solve for the heat transfer rate:

    • \( Q + \dot{m} h_1 = W + \dot{m} h_2 \)

  • Rearrange and simplify, assuming changes in kinetic and potential energy are negligible:

    • \( Q = W + \dot{m} c_p (T_2 - T_1) \)

  • Substitute known values:

    • \( Q = -21~kW + \left( \frac{ 19 }{60}~kg/s \right) \times 1.0047~kJ/(kg \cdot K) \times (421 - 251) \)

  • Calculate \( Q \).

  • Substitute the given values:

    • \( \Delta S_{air} = ( \frac{ 19 }{60}~kg/s ) \times 1.0047~kJ/(kg \cdot K) \times \ln ( \frac{ 421 }{ 251 } ) \)
