      Solving for all forces:

    • \( \Sigma{M}_{Bolt} =0 \) \( \implies \) \( L_2(F_n)-L_1(F)=0 \)
    • The normal force the ball exerts is \( F_n = \frac{L_1(F)}{L_2} \)

    • \( \Sigma{F}_{y} =0 \) \( \implies \) Bolt\( _y - F_n - F =0 \)
    • Plugging in \( F_n \) and solving for Bolt\( _y \) we get Bolt\( _y = F(1+\frac{sin(90-\theta)L_1}{L_2}) \)

    • \( \Sigma{F}_{x} =0 \) \( \implies \) Bolt\( _x + F_ncos(90-\theta)=0 \)
    • Plugging in \( F_n \) and solving for Bolt\( _x \) we get Bolt\( _x = \frac{Fcos(90-\theta)L_1}{L_2} \)
