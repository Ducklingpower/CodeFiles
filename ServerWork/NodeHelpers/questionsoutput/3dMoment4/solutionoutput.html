Level 0

Identify which forces will have an effect along each of the axis

Level 1

Any force that is parallel to the axises will not induce a moment about that axis.

Level 2

    Forces that have a moment about each axis:
  • X-axis: \( F_R,F_P,F_P, \) and \( F_G \)
  • Y-axis: \( F_G \) and \( F_G \)
  • Z-axis: \( F_P,F_G, \) and \( F_R \)
    • Now that you have the forces that can cause a moment about each axis, you can multiply them by their distance away from the axis to fidn the moment.

Level 3

    Find Moment about eahc axis.
  • Moment X-axis: \( \Sigma{M}_x=(L_1)(F_R) + (L_1)(F_P)+(L_1)(F_G)-(L_1)(F_P) \)
  • Moment Y-axis: \( \Sigma{M}_y = -(L_2)(F_G)-(L_1)(F_G) \)
  • Moment Z-axis: \( \Sigma{M}_z = (L_2)(F_P)-(L_1)(F_G)-(L_1)(F_R) \)
    • Note: counter clockwise rotation is denoted as positve(+) while clockwise rotation is negative (-)
