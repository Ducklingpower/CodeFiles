Level 1

Conservation of moment:

  • \( v_B m_B + v m = (m_B + m ) v_f ~\implies ~ \frac{v_B m_b + 0 }{m_b + m} = v_f =6.693069306930693~m/s \)

    • Where \( v_f \) is the velocity of the block and the bullet after collision.

    To find the time, compute the impule moemnt principle.

    Level 2

      Impule moment principle, \( m v_1 + \Sigma \int F dt = m v_2 \)

      Applying in the Y directiuon:

    • \( 0 + (N (t) + mg (t)) =0 ~\implies~ N = mg \)

      • Applying in the X directiuon:

    • \( m v_f - (\mu N t) = 0 ~implies~ t = \frac{m v_f}{\mu N} \)

      • Plugging in N we can solve for t:

    • \( t = \frac{m v_f}{\mu (mg)} ~\implies~ \frac{v_f}{\mu g} = 2.624115622571431 ~s \)

      • Now we can find the total distance traveled.
