Level 1

    Writing equations of motion for the horizontal and verticle componets.

    Horizontal: \( v = \frac{D}{t} \) \( \implies \) \( vcos(\theta + \phi) = \frac{Dcos(\theta)}{t} \) \( \implies \) \( D = \frac{vcos(\theta+\phi)t}{cos(\theta)} \)

    Verticle: \( D = vt+\frac{1}{2}at^2 \) \( \implies \) \( Dsin(\theta) = vsin(\theta+\phi)t-\frac{1}{2}gt^2 \)
