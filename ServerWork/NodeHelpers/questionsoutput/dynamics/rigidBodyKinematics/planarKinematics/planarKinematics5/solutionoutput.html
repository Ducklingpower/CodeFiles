Level 0

The first step is to write out the equation of relative velocity and identify your unknowns.

Level 1

Reltive motion equation: \( v_c = v_b + \omega _{BC} \) x \( r_{CB} \)

There will not be any velocity in the x direction since the slider is constrained to only move in the verticale.
  • pluggig in we get: \( -v \hat{j} = (-L_1 \omega_{AB})\hat{j} + \omega _{BC} \) x \( [L_2 cos(\theta) \hat{i} + L_2 sin(\theta) \hat{j}] \)
  • Simplifying we get \( : -v \hat{j} = (-L_1 \omega_{AB})\hat{j} + \omega _{BC} L_2 cos(\theta) \hat{j} - \omega _{BC} L_2 sin(\theta) \hat{i}] \)

    • Breaking up vector equation in to components:

  • \( -v\hat{j} = L_2 cos(\theta) - L_1 \omega{AB} \)
  • \( 0 = -L_2sin(\theta) \omega{BC} \)

    • Now we have 2 equations and 2 unknowns.

    Level 2
