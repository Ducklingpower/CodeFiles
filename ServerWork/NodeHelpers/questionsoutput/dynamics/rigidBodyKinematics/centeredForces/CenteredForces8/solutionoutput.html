Level 0

The first step is to draw a free-body diagram of the system so that we are able to sum the forces.

Level 1

Summing forces:

  • \( \Sigma{F}_y ~\implies ~m_a a = m_ag - T ~\implies~ T = m_a(g-a) ~\implies~ T = m_a(g-\alpha r) \)

    • This force analysis in the y direction is with respect to mass A.

  • \( \Sigma{M}_G ~\implies ~ I_G \alpha = T r \)
    • Here we have 2 equations and two unknowns: \( T \) and \( \alpha \)

    Level 2

      Solving we get:

    • \( \alpha = \frac{m_a r g}{mk^2 + m_a r^2} = 6.5053050397878 ~rad/s^2 \)
