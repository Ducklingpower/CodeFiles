Level 0

The first step is to draw a free body diagram of the horzontal beam. Take note that there is both tangental acceloratrion and normal acceloration.

Level 1

Summing forces in normal and tangental directions:

  • \( \Sigma{F}_n ~\implies ~\omega^2L_1 m = N_1 + N_2 - m g cos(\theta) \)

    • Here \( N_1 \) and \( N_2 \) are the normal reaction force of the two support beams.

  • \( \Sigma{F}_t ~\implies ~a_t m = mgsin(\theta) \)
  • \( \Sigma{M}_G ~\implies ~ 0 = N_2 cos(\theta) \frac{L_2}{2} - N_1 cos(\theta) \frac{L_2}{2} \)
    • Here we have 3 equations and three unknowns: \( N_1,~N_2, \) and \( a_t \)

    Level 2

      Solving:

    • From moment equation: \( N_1 = N_2 \)
    • From \( \Sigma{F}_n \) equation: \( N_1 = \frac{\omega^2 L_1 m +mg cos(\theta)}{2} = 35.96027258818964~ kN \)
    • From \( \Sigma{F}_t \) equation: \( a_t = sin(\theta) g = 4.904999999999999 ~ m/s^2 \)
