Level 0

The first step is to draw a free body diagram of the system so that we are able to sum the forces.

Level 1

Summing forces:

  • \( \Sigma{F}_n ~\implies ~\omega^2 L m = N_x \). At the instant the fly wheel is releases angulare velocity is zero so, \( N_x = 0 \)
  • \( \Sigma{F}_t ~\implies ~(\alpha L) m = mg - N_y \)
  • \( \Sigma{M}_G ~\implies ~ I_G \alpha = N_y L \)
    • Here we have 2 equations and two unknowns: \( N_y \) and \( \alpha \)

    Level 2

      Solving we get:

    • \( N_y = \frac{mg} {\frac{L^2}{k^2} +1 } = 16.548531099344032~N \)
    • \( \alpha = \frac{gL}{L^2 + k^2} = 6.357543665533866~rad/s^2 \)
