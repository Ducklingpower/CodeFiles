The first step is to draw a free-body diagram. there should be four forces in your FBD.

Level 1

Free body diagram of the system:

  • weight force pulling down from G
  • normal force at A pushing up 2x
  • normal force at B pushing up 2x
  • Force pushing left

    • Now that we have the free body diagram we can compute the equations of motion and solve for the unknowns.

      \( \Sigma{F_x} = ma ~\implies~ F = ma ~\implies~ a = \frac{F}{m} \)

      \( \Sigma{F_y} = 0 ~\implies~ 0 = 2N_a + 2N_B - w ~\implies~ N_a = \frac{w}{2} - N_b \)

      \( \Sigma{M_G} = 0 ~\implies ~ 0 = 2N_BL_2 + F(L_4 + L_3) - 2N_A L_1 \)

      We have 2 equations and 2 unknowns, we can solve for \( N_A \) and \( N_B \) now.
