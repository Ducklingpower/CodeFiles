  • \( \Sigma{\Phi} = 0 \)
    • To compute the change in twist in each section we have to find the torque acting on each segment.

    Level 2

      Segment \( L_3 \) = \( -M_B \)

      Segment \( L_1 \) = \( -M_B+\tau_2-\tau_1 \)

    • \( \Sigma{\Phi} = 0 ~\implies \frac{-M_B ~ L_3}{J ~G} + \frac{(-M_B+\tau_2) ~ L_3}{J ~G} +\frac{(-M_B+\tau_2-\tau_1) ~ L_3}{J ~G} = 0 \)
