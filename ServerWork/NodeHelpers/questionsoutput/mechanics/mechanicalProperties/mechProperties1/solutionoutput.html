Level 0

Identify what properties you have and try to make an equation that solves for the modulus of elasticity.

Level 1

You can use these three equations: \( \sigma = \frac{F}{A},~\sigma = \epsilon E, ~ \epsilon = \frac{\delta L}{L} \)

Level 2

    Using the equations we can put them together to make one equation: \( \frac{F}{A} = \frac{\delta L}{L} E ~\implies E = \frac{F~L}{A~\delta L} \) Now you can plug in all of your variables. It might be good to convert Kn to N and mm to m. Then convert pa to Mpa after plugging in.
