Level 0

Try to calculate the original length of corner CB and CB'(deformed CB)

Level 1

Calculating CB and CB':

  • \( L_{CB} = \sqrt{L_1^2+L_2^2} \)
  • \( L'_{CB} = \sqrt{(L_1+L_3)^2+(L_2-L_4)} \)
    • Now try and calculate the normal strain.

    Level 2

      Calculating normal strain:

    • strain = \( \frac{L'_{CB}-L_{CB} }{L_{CB} } \)

      • Try to use the deformed shape to calculate the shear strain at points A and C

    Level 3

      Shear strain at point A:

    • Strain at point A = \( tan^{-1}(\frac{L_4}{L_1+L_3}) \)

      • Shear strain at point C:

      Let \( \theta_1 \) be the angle between the dashed line CB and the horizontal. We can calculate this angle to be:
    • \( \theta_1 = tan^{-1}(\frac{L_2-L_4}{L_1+L_3}) \)

      • Let \( \theta_2 \) be the angle ACB, we can calculate that by:
    • \( \theta_2 = tan^{-1}(\frac{L_1}{L_2}) \)

      • Finally, we can calculate the shear strain at corner C to be:
    • shear strain at C = \( \frac{\pi}{2}-(\theta_1+\theta_2) \)
