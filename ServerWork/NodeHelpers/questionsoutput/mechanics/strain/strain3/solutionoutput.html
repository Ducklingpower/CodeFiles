Compute the lengths of AC and AC compressed.

Level 1

Computing lengths of AC:

  • Before compression: \( L_{AC} = \sqrt{L^2+L^2} \)
  • After compression: \( L'_{AC} = \sqrt{(L_2+L_2+L)^2+(L-L_3)^2} \)
    • Now try and Compute the strain along AC.

    Level 2

      Computing strain along AC:
    • strain AC = \( \frac{L'{AC}-L_{AC} } {L_{AC} } = 0.00391557986728812~ (\frac{mm}{mm}) \)

      • Now try and compute the shear strain at point E

    Level 3

      We can compute the new angle between the positive x' and y' axis to be:

    • \( \theta = 2tan(\frac{L_2+L_2+L1}{L1-L_3}) \)
      • Making sure our \( \theta \) is in radians we can compute shear strain.
    • Shear strain = \( \frac{\pi}{2}-\theta = -0.030286373804167654~(Radians) \)
