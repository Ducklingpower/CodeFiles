Level 0

Compute the change in length of wires AB and DC to give the angle theta.

Level 1

Computing \( \delta \) lengths of wires:

  • \( \delta L_{AB} = tan(\theta)L \)
  • \( \delta L_{DC} = tan(\theta)(2L+L) \)
    • Using the change in lengths of your wires try to calculate the strain.

    Level 2

      Calculating strain:
    • Strain\( _{AB} = \frac{\delta L_{AB} }{\frac{L}{2} } \)

    • Strain\( _{CD} = \frac{\delta L_{CD} }{L} \)

    Level 3

      Solutions:

      Strain\( _{AB} = 0.0006283185100471087 \)

      Strain\( _{CD} = 0.000942477765070663 \)
