Level 2

    Equations that discribe all of the forces:
  • \( \Sigma{F}_x = 0 \implies A_x-F_P =0 \)
  • \( \Sigma{F}_y = 0 \implies A_y+F_R =0 \)
  • \( \Sigma{F}_z = 0 \implies A_z-F_G =0 \)
    • Moment equations (about point A):
  • \( \Sigma{M}_x = 0 \implies M_x-(L_3+L_2)(F_R)-(L_1)(F_G) =0 \)
  • \( \Sigma{M}_y = 0 \implies M_y-(L_2)(F_P)-(L_2)(F_G) =0 \)
  • \( \Sigma{M}_z = 0 \implies M_z =0 \)
    • With all of these equations you are able to solve for all of the reaction forces at point A.
