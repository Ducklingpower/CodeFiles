A rectangular block of plastic material is described as having the following dimensions: length $L = {{params.length}}$ mm, width $W = {{params.width}}$ mm, and height $H = {{params.height}}$ mm. Its lower face is glued to a bench, and a force of $F = {{params.force}}$ N is applied to the upper face, resulting in the upper face moving $\delta = {{params.displacement}}$ mm relative to the lower face.

  1. Determine the shear stress on the upper face in {{params.unitsStress}}.
  2. Determine the shear strain on the upper face.

Assume the deformation is uniform.
