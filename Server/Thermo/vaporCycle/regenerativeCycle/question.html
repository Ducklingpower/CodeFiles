Consider a regenerative vapor power cycle with one open feedwater heater. Steam enters the turbine at a pressure of $\mathbf{P_1} = {{params.P1}}$ MPa and a temperature of $\mathbf{T_1} = {{params.T1}}^oC$, and expands to $\mathbf{P_2} = {{params.P2}}$ MPa, where some of the steam is extracted and diverted to the open feedwater heater operating at $\mathbf{P_2} = {{params.P2}}$ MPa. The remaining steam expands through the second-stage turbine to the condenser pressure of $\mathbf{P_3} = {{params.P3}}$ MPa. Saturated liquid exits the open feedwater heater at $\mathbf{P_2} = {{params.P2}}$ MPa.
