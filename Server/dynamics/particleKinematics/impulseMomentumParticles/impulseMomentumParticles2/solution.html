The first step is to apply the conservation of momentum to the blocks.

Applying conservation of momentum to the blocks we get:

  • $m_A v_A + m_B v _B = (m_A + m_B) v_f$
    • Solving for $v_f$ we get:
  • $\frac{m_A v_A + m_B v _B}{m_A + m_B} = v_f = {{correct_answers.v}}~m/s$

    • To solve for the average force during the collision we have to use impulse momentum equation.

      Apling impulse moment equation to block A:

    • $mv_i + \Sigma \int F_x dt = m v_f$
    • $(m_A v_A) - F_{avg}(t) = m_A v_f$

      • Solving for $F_{avg}$ we get :

    • $F_{avg} = \frac{m_A (v_A-v_f)}{t} = {{correct_answers.f}}~ N$
